## Posting Schedule & Best Practices

### LinkedIn Optimal Times
- **Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Time:** 8:00-10:00 AM EST
- **Worst:** Weekends for business content

### First-Hour Engagement Protocol
1. Post content
2. Immediately engage with 5-10 other posts in your niche
3. Reply to every comment in first 60 minutes
4. Ask follow-up questions in replies
5. LinkedIn rewards early engagement velocity

### Hashtag Strategy (3-5 per post)
- #AI (broad reach)
- #ClaudeCode or #AnthropicClaude (niche)
- #BuildInPublic (community)
- #AITools or #ProductDevelopment (relevant)
- #TechFounders (audience)
